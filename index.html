I am an Assistant Teaching Professor in the Department of Computer Science at UCSB. I received a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Virginia, and an MTech in Computer Science from IIT Bombay.

I am passionate about teaching and broadening participation in Computer Science, and supporting undergraduate research in Cyber Physical Systems and CS Education.
